<rule id="include" name="WHAT TO EXTRACT">

- Unique place names — locations specific to this fictional world.
- Unique items, skills, organizations, races — only if unique to this world (not generic concepts).
</rule>

<rule id="exclude" name="WHAT TO IGNORE">
- Onomatopoeia and interjections — sound effects, emotional exclamations.
